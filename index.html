About Godrej Doddaballapur

Godrej Plots Doddaballapur is a premium residential plotted development by Godrej Properties, spread across 48 acres in the serene surroundings of North Bangalore. Designed for modern living, this project offers well-planned plots that let you build your dream home amidst lush green landscapes and world-class infrastructure.

Strategically located near Kempegowda International Airport, KIADB Industrial Area, and major city hubs, it promises both exceptional lifestyle convenience and excellent investment potential. With RERA approval in progress, Godrej Plots Doddaballapur presents a rare opportunity to own land in a secure, well-developed township from one of India's most trusted real estate brands.
